Evolution Of Music Basti

Music Basti is an initiative of Integrated Development Education Association (IDEA)

From creating fun musical experiences for vulnerable children in 2008, Music Basti has matured into an organisation focussed on creating a space for personal growth and nurturing key life-skills today

Music Basti has formed a (musical) community that comprises its NGO partners, donor partners, teaching artists, volunteers, musicians,and countless supporters

Music Basti has grown from a small group of volunteers in 2008 to a vibrant team of over 30 full and part time staff, who are assisted by a large number of volunteers. Beyond our core team, 20+ teaching artists join Music Basti every year in implementing the project, playing the most important role in the studentsâ€™ learning and experience.
